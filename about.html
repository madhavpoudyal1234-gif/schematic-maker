About Schematic Maker

Schematic Maker is a specialized AI tool designed for educators, students, and scientific illustrators.

Our goal is to simplify the creation of professional examination papers by transforming rough, hand-drawn sketches into clean, digital, vector-style diagrams. Whether it is a physics pulley system, a geometric prism, or a biological cell, our engine ensures a high-resolution, stark-white result suitable for print.
